Lázio Chess Club

At Lázio Chess Club, our mission is to foster a love of chess by developing strategic and intellectual skills in our members, from beginners to experienced players. We believe that chess is a powerful tool for cognitive and personal development, as well as being an excellent form of fun and socialization.

“A book cannot by itself teach how to play. It can only serve as a guide, and the rest must be learned by experience.” - Jose Capablanca, "Chess Fundamentals" (1921)
